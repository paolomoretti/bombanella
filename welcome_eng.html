Bombanella Soundscapes is a custom analogue and digital recording studio founded in 2005 by Davide Cristiani in Maranello, deep in in the Italian countryside but close to Modena and Bologna and easy accessible both by plane and by train (2 hours train from Milan and 30 minutes from Bologna).
In a relaxed, rural environment Bombanella Soundscapes is the ideal studio for all your projects.
The wooden main recording space covers 100 square meters (approximately 1080 square feet). Equipment includes custom, unique and vintage recording gear working with a custom Souncraft recording console and a Telefunken 24 channels 2" tape machine plus a special custom-tube-digital System.
Depending on your needs, two sound engineers are available: Davide Cristiani for recording, mixing and mastering and Max Micelli (who designed and built all our custom equipment) for technical support.
